Our Doctors

We have over 40 doctors who commit their Saturdays mornings to the Rahma Community Clinic. They come from very diverse medical background:

  • Internal Medicine
  • Family Practice
  • Cardiology
  • Oncology
  • Neurology
  • Gastroenterology
  • Ophthalmology


Dr. Irshad Jafri specializes in Gastroenterology and Internal Medicine. He went to medical school at Sindh Medical College in Karachi, Pakistan. He moved to the United States and completed Masters in Public Health at Emory University, Atlanta, GA; completed his Internal Medicine Residency at Hennepin County Medical Center, Minneapolis, MN; finished his Fellowship in Gastroenterology at University of Massachusetts Medical Center in Worcester, MA.

Currently, Dr. Jafri is the Department Head of Gastroenterology and Hepatology at Regions Hospital and Healthpartners Medical Group in the Greater Twin Cities area. He also visits the Department of Medicine, University of Minnesota Medical School as an Assistant ProfessorHe is fluent in Urdu and Bengali.

Dr. Jafar Hasan is a fellowship trained pediatric ophthalmology with expertise in the medical and surgical care of eye diseases in children. Dr. Hasan specializes in strabismus (eye muscle misalignment), amblyopia (commonly called lazy eye), tear duct obstruction, and other pediatric conditions.

Dr. Hasan received his medical degree from the University of Kentucky College of Medicine graduating with honors. He completed his internship and his Ophthalmology residency at the University of Kentucky. Dr. Hasan then received specialty training in pediatric ophthalmology and strabismus at Baylor College of Medicine - Texas Children's Hospital, which is consistently ranked as one of the top pediatric hospitals in the country.



Dr. Bushra Dar comes in very helpful when certain female patients want to be seen by a female doctor only. Along with women's health, her interests also include diabetes management, thyroid disorders, asthma, and mental health. She specializes in Family Medicine and practices in Maple Grove, MN.

Native of Pakistan, she went to medical school at Sindh Medical College in Karachi, and then proceeded to University of Minnesota Medical School, and has been practicing since the mid 90's. She is fluent in Urdu.
