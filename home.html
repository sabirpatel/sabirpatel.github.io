Welcome to the Rahma Community Clinic!

Located less than 2 miles away from Mall of America in Bloomington, MN, Rahma Community Clinic is a free facility run by volunteers only.

Muslim Physicians of Minnesota in collaboration with Muslim Community Center oversee the medical operations of the clinic.

Open Saturday mornings only from 9:00 am - 12:00 pm, Rahma sees patients regardless of race, color, or religion.

Everyone is Welcome!!

Services offered are:

  • Free doctor's consultation and visit
  • Written prescriptions
  • Discounted laboratory tests (via a third party vendor)
  • X-Rays (via a third party vendor)
  • Scheduled Specialist consultation
